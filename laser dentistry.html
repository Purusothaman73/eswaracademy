Laser Dentistry

Dr. Arun T. Kulandaivelu educates participants in the revolutionary field of lasers in dentistry and hygiene. Students learn through multi-media slide and video presentations as well as patient demonstration.

By the end of the course, participants will be ready to go back into their practices and institute the most common soft tissue laser procedures and periodontal therapies with confidence.

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN...

Upon completion of the training program you will be able to:

  • Differentiate laser light from natural light
  • Review safe and effective set-up, operation and maintenance of the laser systems.
  • Incorporate lasers into your dental practices
  • Identify & use specific wavelengths and operating parameters of lasers.
  • Specify factors which influence the effects of laser on biological tissue
  • What is involved in a typical laser appointment
  • Soft tissue and periodontal applications of lasers
