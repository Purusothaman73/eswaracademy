Dental Care

Orthodontics


At Eswar Dental Hospital, we create confident, perfectly aligned smiles for patients of all ages. Our orthodontics specialists use advanced techniques, comfortable appliances, and personalized care to correct misalignments, close gaps, and ensure a healthy bite—discreetly and effectively.

What We Offer:

  • Comprehensive orthodontic assessments for children, teens, and adults
  • Metal and ceramic braces for reliable, predictable results
  • Invisible clear aligners (like Invisalign) for a wire-free, aesthetic option
  • Retainers and space maintainers for long-term oral stability
  • Early intervention treatments to guide proper jaw growth and development

Why Choose Eswar Dental Hospital for Orthodontics?

We blend expert clinical skill with the latest digital technology for precise planning and results. Our team explains each step—so you understand your options, expected timelines, and investment. Treatment is tailored to your lifestyle and goals, with regular progress reviews to ensure everything stays on track.

Smile Transformation, Your Way

Whether you want a subtle adjustment or a dramatic realignment, we make orthodontics easy and approachable. Let us help you achieve a lifetime of healthy, straight teeth and a confident, radiant smile.

Frequently Asked Questions — Orthodontics

The Indian Dental Association recommends children have their first orthodontic evaluation by age 7. Early assessment allows us to detect issues with tooth and jaw development and recommend timely intervention if needed.

We offer traditional metal braces, ceramic (tooth-colored) braces, and clear aligners. Each option has unique benefits for comfort, appearance, and treatment needs—your orthodontist will help you choose the best fit.

Treatment time varies depending on age, severity of misalignment, and chosen appliance. Most cases range from 12 to 24 months, but some may see results in as little as 6 months with clear aligners.
